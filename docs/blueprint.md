# **App Name**: AuraForge

## Core Features:

- 3D Cinematic Scroll Experience: An immersive, scroll-triggered 3D environment that transitions between 'scenes' as described in the brief, utilizing WebGL/Three.js.
- Dynamic Logo and Tagline Reveal: Initiate with a dark screen, reveal the Mix Aura logo and tagline 'We Engineer Digital Influence' through animated particles, glowing effects, and a breathing aura.
- Interactive Service Visualizations: Showcase Digital Marketing and Web Development services through abstract 3D structures with flowing energy and subtle glitch effects, responding to user interaction.
- Holographic Portfolio Display: Present project case studies as glowing holographic portals that expand into a full-screen cinematic view upon selection.
- Futuristic Contact Panel: A 'digital command center' style contact form with glowing input fields and an animated submit button emitting a purple energy pulse.
- AI-Powered Copy Resonance Tool: An interactive tool that generates bespoke marketing copy suggestions based on conceptual keywords, demonstrating Mix Aura's technological mastery in crafting digital influence.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A vibrant electric neon purple (#C41BFD) symbolizing creative energy and technological prowess. This bold hue stands out against the dark scheme.
- Background Color: A deep, almost void-black with a subtle purple undertone (#100E11), providing a canvas that is both premium and expansive, reflecting dominance and futuristic aesthetics.
- Accent Color: A bright, controlled blue-violet (#A38AFF) that creates dynamic contrast and highlights interactive elements, akin to subtle energy pulses.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), chosen for its contemporary, techy, and slightly unconventional aesthetic, aligning with the futuristic and intelligent brand mood.
- Code Font: 'Source Code Pro' (monospace), for any display of technical code snippets, maintaining consistency with the technological focus.
- Rely on custom-designed, minimalist geometric shapes and dynamic particle effects rendered in 3D. Avoid standard icon sets, favoring glowing lines, subtle glitches, and flowing energy visuals as functional iconography.
- Full-screen, immersive 3D viewport, driven by scroll for scene transitions. Content appears dynamically within this 3D space, rather than adhering to traditional grid layouts, creating a non-template, cinematic flow.
- Extensive use of custom 3D animations, particle systems, and shader effects. Smooth parallax scrolling, interactive hover states with electric effects, soft warps for transitions, and controlled glow animations are integral to conveying dominance and creative power through 'controlled chaos' and micro-interactions.
